Welcome to the ECMAScript 6 presentation

These slides were developed with a goal to learn ECMAScript 6 (ES2015) and some ECMAScript 7 (ES2016) features. They are self-explained. I have tried my best to summarize all most used features and present them in understandable, simple, but deeply explained portions. Feel free to use them in your own presentations.
